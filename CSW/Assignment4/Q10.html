Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, the Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Qutub Minar

Standing tall in Delhi, Qutub Minar is the world's tallest brick minaret and a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Hawa Mahal

Located in Jaipur, Hawa Mahal, or the "Palace of Winds," is known for its unique facade with 953 small windows.

Red Fort

A historic fort in Delhi, the Red Fort served as the main residence of Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

Gateway of India

Situated in Mumbai, the Gateway of India is a majestic arch that marks a significant colonial landmark.
